Winter: Dec, 2022

If you are someone that likes or loves Pokémon, well I can help you with that. For this piece, someone I follow on Instagram hosted a small event to celebrate the final arc in the Pokémon franchise with Ash Ketchum as the main protagonist for over 25 seasons of the anime, and who happened to be become a ©Pokémon Master and the Monarch (World Champion) at the end of Pokémon Journey. For this small event, the host wanted people to created a team with our OCs from One Piece. However, the host asked us to create the team with Pokémon that match with our OCs personality and if they had a Devil Fruit Power (Powers in One Piece they get from eatting these fruits). I picked my OC Sunet, since she is one of my main OCs from the One Piece OCs I have. When it came to this piece, I had to think about witch Pokémons made the most sense to her. When I started to think about it, two Pokémon came to mind witch was Flareon and Nine Tails because they are fire type and they go with her personality. When you start a Pokémon game from any generation, you have to pick from three starter Pokémon. Fire, Water and Grass are the three types that you pick from. For this, I knew that Sunset, would pick a Fire Type starter. I looked at the all the fire type from the franchise, the one that I picked was Scorbunny from generation 10. Scorbunny evolves to Raboot and evolves to Cinderace. For the other three Pokémon, the hosted wanted us to have at least one Mythical or Legendary Pokémon. But, I picked one of each, one Mythical and one Legendary, and those two Pokémon were Dialga for the Legendary and Celebi for the Mythical since they match Sunset's Devil Fruit Power. For the last one, I started to think about the new generation of Pokémon and we are introduce Paradox Pokémon from pass and future Pokémon. For this I went with a future Paradox Pokémon, Iron Valiant (visual traits from both Gardevoir and Gallade) since this one is the newer Pokémon, and it is based off from the future.
